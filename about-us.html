    • About us!

    Enigma Bolt System since 2002. is professionally engaged in the production of container and container facilities.

    With advancement in technology, we have come to a series of improvements to the production capacity, the method of transport and installation on remote locations.

    Medium sized company, Enigma Bolt System employs thirty manufacturing workers as well as professional team that creates technology and product development.
    In terms of quality as well as production capacity, EBS occupies a leading position in region.

    Since its establishment in 2002, with over 10,000 units produced, in addition to the domestic market, EBS containers are present in many countries in Europe, Asia, Caribbean Islands, as well as in the United States.
